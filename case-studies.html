16Tech Innovation District, Indianapolis February 27, 2026 7.5 hours

Our first live deployment. Five BLE gateways across a multi-room venue, shared public Wi-Fi, zero advance venue testing. The system captured comprehensive attendee movement data across all sessions, booths, and common areas — surfacing patterns the organizers had never been able to quantify before.

Key Findings

39% no-show rate with a measurable geographic predictor — attendees traveling 50+ miles were 3x more likely to not show up.
$507 in preventable food waste identified from a single event based on actual vs. planned attendance.
Persistent spatial asymmetry between two parallel session rooms — one room consistently at 90%+ capacity while the adjacent room ran at under 25%, a pattern nobody had quantified before.
BLE signal achieved 4.6-second average frequency across all zones with zero dead spots, despite shared public Wi-Fi and no advance venue testing.
Badge-to-badge proximity data captured networking density in the dining area — peaking during lunch with 34 simultaneous interactions detected.
