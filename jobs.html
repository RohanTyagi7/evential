About Evential

Founded in late 2025, Evential is a frictionless event intelligence platform powered by a suite of elegantly designed hardware and software solutions. We don't just help event teams prepare and analyze events—we automate the entire show. From real-time inventory and crowd flow awareness to attendee connection insights and intelligent staff coordination, Evential automates every moving part of a live event.

It predicts bottlenecks before they happen, adapts operations as conditions change, and gives organizers and on-site teams complete visibility. The mission is simple: make events effortless to run so bringing people together is no longer difficult or time consuming.

When it comes to creating seamless event experiences, Evential is the platform you trust.
