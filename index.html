How It Works

From badge tap to sponsor report in five steps.

Set up once. Capture everything automatically. Monetize the data your sponsors already want.

01

Create Your Event

Multi-step wizard guides you through event setup, floor plan design with drag-and-drop booth placement, and attendee CSV import.

02

Deploy BLE Badges

Ship smart BLE badges to your venue. Gateways track attendee movement throughout the space in real-time — zero behavior change required.

03

Watch It Live

See real-time check-ins, booth heatmaps, session attendance, dwell times, and foot traffic patterns as your event unfolds.

04

Auto-Generate Reports

Within 24 hours of event close, sponsors receive comprehensive ROI reports showing booth performance vs. event averages.

05

Sell Qualified Leads

Build and sell lead reports to sponsors — contacts who visited their booth, dwelled 5+ minutes, and opted in to data sharing.

06

Stay Compliant

Movement data is anonymous by default. PII is only linked when attendees explicitly opt in. Built-in GDPR/CCPA templates handle the rest.
