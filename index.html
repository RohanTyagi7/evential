Data & Privacy

Built with data responsibility at the foundation, not bolted on after.

We know the first question any enterprise buyer or legal team asks. Here are the answers.

Attendee Consent

Badges are disclosed as standard event equipment. Organizers control consent language at registration. All spatial data is anonymized and de-identified at the point of collection — no personal movement profiles are created without explicit opt-in.

Data Ownership

Your event data is yours. Evential does not sell, license, or share individual event data with third parties. Aggregated cross-event benchmarks are opt-in only. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can request full deletion at any time.

Compliance

Evential is designed to support GDPR and CCPA compliance obligations. SOC 2 certification is in progress. Our data processing agreements are available on request for enterprise procurement teams. We work alongside your legal and security review.

Common questions: Can attendees opt out? Yes — organizers can configure opt-out at check-in. What if a badge is removed? Tracking stops; no inference is made. Who can see the data? Only authorized event staff through your Evential dashboard. Is location data stored permanently? No — raw location streams are processed and discarded; only aggregated analytics are retained.
