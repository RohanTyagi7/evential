About Evential

What is Evential?

Evential is an event analytics platform built for B2B conference and trade show organizers. It combines BLE-based attendee tracking, automated sponsor ROI reporting, and a qualified lead marketplace into a single dashboard — replacing manual spreadsheets and post-event guesswork with real-time event data analytics.

For organizers: Get real-time operational intelligence — see who checked in, which sessions are full, and where foot traffic is concentrated across your venue.

For sponsors: Automatically receive verified ROI reports showing booth visits, dwell time, and qualified leads within 24 hours of event close.

For venues: Permanent gateway installations turn every booked event into an intelligent, data-rich experience without additional setup.

The event analytics platform works through a network of BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) smart badges and fixed gateway sensors. Badges are distributed to attendees at check-in; gateways installed throughout the venue capture badge signals to track movement, booth engagement, and session attendance in real time.

Unlike manual lead capture tools or post-event survey platforms, Evential's event intelligence platform captures data continuously throughout your event — giving you a complete picture of attendee behavior and sponsor ROI without any manual entry.

How Event Analytics Works

From badge tap to sponsor ROI report in five steps.

Set up once. Capture everything automatically. Monetize the event data your sponsors already want. Here's how Evential's event intelligence platform works end to end.

01
Create Your Event

A multi-step wizard guides you through event setup, floor plan design with drag-and-drop booth placement, and attendee CSV import. Your event analytics configuration is saved and ready to deploy.

02
Deploy BLE Badges

Ship smart BLE badges to your venue. Gateway sensors track attendee movement throughout the space in real time — capturing booth engagement and session attendance with zero behavior change required from attendees.

03
Watch It Live

See real-time check-ins, booth heatmaps, session attendance, dwell times, and foot traffic patterns as your event unfolds. Your event analytics features dashboard updates continuously.

04
Auto-Generate Sponsor ROI Reports

Within 24 hours of event close, sponsors receive comprehensive ROI reports showing booth performance vs. event averages — the kind of verified sponsor ROI tracking data that drives renewals.

05
Sell Qualified Leads

Build and sell lead reports to sponsors — contacts who visited their booth, dwelled 5+ minutes, and opted in to data sharing. Evential's event lead generation marketplace turns attendee engagement into post-event revenue.

06
Stay GDPR Compliant

Attendee movement data is anonymous by default. PII is not linked when attendees opt out at check-in. Built-in country-based GDPR compliant event tracking templates handle the rest automatically.
